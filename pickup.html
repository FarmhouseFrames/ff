Picture Perfect

Every Farmhouse Frames piece is prepared with care and subject to rigorous quality checks before delivery. We treat your chosen images with the same respect as a gallery installation, ensuring that whether your photo is destined for a canvas, a custom clock, or a family puzzle, it meets our professional standards.

Our Boutique Quality Standard

  • Color Accuracy: We verify that the print colors on your canvas or decor item perfectly match the original photography.
  • Material Integrity: We check that puzzles are crisply cut, clocks are perfectly timed, and canvases are stretched tight without warping.
  • Detail Check: Every item is inspected for clarity and sharpness to ensure your image looks stunning at any scale.

Local Pickup

  • Ready Notification: You will receive a personal alert the moment your custom items have passed their final quality check.
  • Flexible Windows: We offer convenient times for you to stop by and collect your orders.
  • Hand-Off: We provide a final look at the piece with you upon pickup to ensure you are thrilled with the result.

Shipping & Handling

  • Secure Packaging: From reinforced corners for canvases to padded boxes for clocks, we ensure everything is immobilized for a safe journey.
  • Real-Time Updates: You will receive a tracking number as soon as your order is dispatched, allowing you to follow your art from our studio to your door.

After Delivery or Shipping

Your experience continues even after your items arrive.

Initial Inspection: We recommend unboxing your decor immediately to ensure everything arrived in pristine condition.

Care Instructions: We provide specific tips on how to clean and maintain your canvases or functional decor to ensure they last for generations.

Customer Support: If you have any questions about hanging your wall art or assembling your puzzles, we are just a message away.
