Pickup & Shipping

Thank you for supporting Farmhouse Frames! Here is everything you need to know about receiving your order.

Local Pickup

Local pickup is available in Kentucky. After placing your order, we will contact you to schedule a convenient pickup time and location.

To arrange pickup, please email us at kristin@farmhouseframes.com.

Shipping

We ship across the United States. Orders are fulfilled within 5–10 business days after purchase. You will receive an email confirmation with tracking information once your order has shipped.

Fulfillment is handled by the Farmhouse Frames owner using Walmart and other trusted suppliers.

Questions?

If you have any questions about your order or shipping timeline, please reach out at kristin@farmhouseframes.com.
