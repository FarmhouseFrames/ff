Pickup & Shipping

Every Farmhouse Frames piece is prepared with care and quality checks before delivery. Below is exactly how local pickup and shipping work, so you know what to expect from order to arrival.

Local Pickup (Cadiz Area)

  • Pickup is available for customers in Cadiz and nearby Trigg County areas.
  • After your order is ready, we contact you directly by email to schedule a pickup window.
  • Bring your order confirmation email at pickup so we can verify and release quickly.
  • Pickup point details are shared privately in your ready-for-pickup message.

Shipping Options

  • Shipping is available for domestic U.S. orders.
  • Rates are calculated using your selected product type and order size.
  • Tracking details are provided after carrier label creation.
  • Large split-canvas sets may ship in multiple boxes for safer transit.

Typical Turnaround

  • Production prep: usually 2-5 business days depending on product type.
  • Local pickup notice: sent as soon as quality check is complete.
  • Shipped orders: typically 3-7 business days in transit after dispatch.
  • Seasonal demand can add extra processing time; we’ll keep you updated.

Packaging & Care

Prints and canvases are packed to protect corners, surfaces, and framing edges. If anything arrives damaged in transit, contact us right away so we can review photos and make it right quickly.
