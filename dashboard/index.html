Build Stages

  1. Provider intake: Save provider metadata to provider_products.
  2. Internal product mapping: Connect storefront products to provider products and derived studio-arrival timeframe.
  3. Photo metadata: Track aspect ratio, orientation, review status, and compatibility.
  4. Print-ready assets: Generate derivatives for print-ready, provider-specific, and web output.
  5. Mockups and imagery: Create mockups and assign them to storefront products.
  6. Order execution: Send paid orders to provider with ship-to-studio flow and track inbound shipment.
  7. Studio branding: Record inspection and branding completion before pickup or shipping.
  8. Delivery completion: Finish via meetup/pickup or studio shipping with final completion audit.
