Certificates

Each finished Farmhouse Frames piece can be paired with clear authenticity and care information so customers know exactly what they purchased.

What the certificate covers

  • Farmhouse Frames product title and format
  • Order reference for the finished piece you purchased
  • Confirmation that the artwork was prepared and sold through Farmhouse Frames

How to request one

If you want a certificate included with a recent order, use the contact page and send your order reference. We can confirm eligibility and let you know the next step.

Care and record keeping

Keep your certificate with your order confirmation for future framing, gifting, or record-keeping needs. It is intended for customer use only and does not expose supplier details.
